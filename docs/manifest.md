# Solleron — manifest (one page)

**What:** Solleron is a community vision for a **self-reliant, community-driven local society** on and around Sollerön. The focus is shared wellbeing, local resilience, and practical cooperation between residents, associations, and local businesses. Full context and sources: [manifest-detail.md](manifest-detail.md).

**Where:** **Sollerön** — largest island in Siljan, Mora kommun — about **1,200** residents on the island (**~1,700** in the wider parish with mainland villages). Strong **agriculture and small enterprise** tradition; **no large industry**; deep **association life** (~65 föreningar) and **Sollerö Sockenförening** (open membership, sockenstämma, arbetsgrupper) already coordinate shared projects. We build **on** that fabric — we do not replace it.

**Why not “self-contained” in the naive sense:** Schools, care, law, and supply chains stay **national/regional**. The goal is stronger local capability, trust, and resilience — not isolation.

**What we learn elsewhere:** [**One Small Town**](https://www.onesmalltown.org/landing_page.php) — voluntary contribution and community coordination; take principles, not mechanics. [**Teal / Reinventing Organizations**](https://www.reinventingorganizations.com/) — distributed authority, trust, and purpose. [**Det nordiska sättet**](BRAAB%20e-bok.pdf) (Bo Andersson) — samråd, procedurrättvisa, and folkrörelse norms. [**NewEarthX**](https://newearthx.com) was reviewed; **it is not a good fit for the Sollerön initiative right now** — different scale, legal framing, and product model (institutional real-asset / token exchange) than parish-level civic coordination. Rationale: [manifest-detail.md](manifest-detail.md) §3.4.

**Principles in short:** legitimacy through local dialogue; complement existing föreningar and local firms; transparent and fair decisions; practical solidarity; stewardship of local land and culture; phased implementation with clear accountability; strengthen preparedness and long-term resilience.
